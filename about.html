About Blaqink Holdings

Leading provider of premium industrial equipment and solutions for diverse sectors across the nation.

Our Company

Blaqink Holdings is a dynamic, ambitious multi-disciplinary entity. The company is a Level 1 BBBEE that has robust relationships with some of the best industry experts with an aim of attracting more and best talent in all its spheres of operation in its pursuit of excellence.

Blaqink Holdings is the exclusive sales partner for Bundor Valves and AirTOP Pneumatic equipment in South Africa. We are official distributors of Festo Pneumatic and Cromwell Tools and many other top component brands in the industry.
